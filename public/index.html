Welcome!

This website is a cool way to see your travel history on TfL. When you add stations, the map comes to life! As you add more stations on a line, the parts between them light up, too.


You can get your journey data from the last 8 weeks from the TfL website. All you have to do is log in and click the export button to get your journey history as a CSV file. Drag and drop onto the page, or upload using the option in the sidebar.


As you begin filling in your map, a URL that's unqiue to you will be available. It changes every time you add a new station so make sure to copy it and save it somewhere before you go.


Want it to be more memorable? Click generate in the sidebar and a four-word phrase will be created to take you back to where you were.


Note: Each phrase or URL will only create the exact map that it was made for. As you add new stations the URL will change. Make sure to generate a new one once you've added your changes.
