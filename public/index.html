Welcome!


This is a fun website to help you visualise your history on TfL. Starting with a faded map, once you begin to add stations their names will light up. As you add more stations on a line, the segments in-between will become visible, too.


TfL let you export the last 8 weeks of journey data from their website. Simply log in and click on the button to export your jouney history as a CSV file. If you don't have an account, but have an Oyster/contactless card you use for journeys, just sign up and follow the steps to link them.


Using the tool in the bottom-left corner, you can upload your exported CSV file, or input stations manually.



N.B.: Syncing between this device and your others is currently unavailable. All data is stored in the browser's localStorage. Support for accounts and cross-device syncing will come in the near future.
